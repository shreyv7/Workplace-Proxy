Google Agent Labs Hackathon 2026
Build Intelligent AI Agents with Google ADK, Qdrant & Lyzr
Overview
The Google Agent Labs Hackathon 2026 is a hands-on AI Agent building challenge designed for students, developers, innovators, and AI enthusiasts who want to explore the future of Agentic AI.
Participants will learn how to build intelligent AI agents powered by Google's Agent Development Kit (ADK), Qdrant's vector memory infrastructure, and Lyzr's agent orchestration capabilities. The hackathon focuses on real-world problem solving through autonomous agents capable of reasoning, memory management, retrieval, automation, and workflow execution.
The event begins with a live workshop where participants will learn the fundamentals of building AI agents and then immediately start building their solutions during the hackathon phase.
Whether you are new to AI Agents or already building advanced agentic systems, this hackathon provides an opportunity to learn, build, network, and compete.
Theme
Building the Future of AI Agents
Design and develop AI agents that can think, remember, retrieve knowledge, automate workflows, and solve real-world challenges using modern agentic technologies.
